Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Carnberries, and Brie

Wow your tastebuds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Ingredients

For the Focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra vergin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoon olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off wtih a paring knife if desired, thought it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh of frozen cranberries

Method

1. Make the focaccia dought

in the medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix togerther all dough Ingredient until the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the dough, though I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
